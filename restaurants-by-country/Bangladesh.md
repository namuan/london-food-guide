# Bangladeshi Restaurants in London

## Amar Gaon
**Location:** Brick Lane

**Comments:**
- "Bangladeshi here. Amar Gaon in Brick Lane serves home-style food. I usually go there and get lots to takeaway when I'm missing authentic food."

## Pran
**Location:** Whitechapel

**Comments:**
- "Pran in Whitechapel is excellent for snacks and sweets that are specifically Bengali too."
