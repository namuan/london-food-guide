## Cantonese Restaurant (Forest Hill)
**Location:** Forest Hill

**Comments:**
- "Best ever Cantonese restaurant in Forest Hill that also specializes in Sichuan cuisine. Their food is really authentic, very different from other generic Chinese restaurant/takeaways. Their fried rice dishes are on a whole other level too plus alot of their dishes are made from scratch down to the sauces, dumplings and condiments."
- "I've been there on multiple occasions! Really affordable as well."
