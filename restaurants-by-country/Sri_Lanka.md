# Sri Lankan Restaurants in London

## Everest
**Location:** Lewisham

**Comments:**
- "My parents (Sri Lankan Tamil) will go to Everest in Lewisham to get food if they cba to cook."

## Hoppers
**Location:** Multiple locations (Soho area)

**Comments:**
- "Hoppers - They are a SriLankan but their food is exactly same as my home state 'Kerala'(in India)."
- "They're opening a new one with a more South Indian focused menu soon!"
- "Hoppers - a good mix of Srilankan & South Indian cuisine. Go for the Lamb karis/curry & couple of different hoppers. Never dissapoints."

## Kolamba
**Location:** Soho

**Comments:**
- "Kolamba in Soho - Its SriLankan however their food is so close to South Indian food and interesting to try new flavours thats close to Indian food so definitely give it a try. Nalini Fish cutlets, hoppers with kiri hodhi, Aunty Mo's Chatti Roast are all out of the world. üòç"

## Rambutan
**Location:** Camden Market

**Comments:**
- "Rambutan in Camden Market - another Srilankan but has a lot of South Indian food as they are similar. Its a new find and thank god I gave it a shot lol. Their cocktails, Arak are good too, if you fancy some drinks. Gundu dosas, butter roti with mutton curry - we literally devoured this lol."

## Gaana
**Location:** Rayners Lane

**Comments:**
- "In terms of Sri Lankan food, would recommend Machan's Kitchen in Clerkenwell. Food is top for a very reasonable price. Honourable mentions to Hopppers and Kolamba too in Central London.
