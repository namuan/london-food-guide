# Venezuelan Restaurants in London

## Conuco
**Location:** Oval

**Comments:**
- "Conuco in Oval is some of the best Venezuelan food I've had in London"
- "Their arepas are legit. Fave lunch snack"

## Liqui Liqui
**Location:** Not specified

**Comments:**
- "Venezuelan here. Liqui Liqui https://www.liqui-liqui.com/liqui-liqui-venezuelan-restaurant-in-london. It is the most authentic I've had. A number of items on the menu are from the north west of the country, especially Maracaibo (where my mum is from)."
