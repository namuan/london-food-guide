# Colombian Restaurants in London

## Leños y Carbón
**Location:** Elephant and Castle

**Comments:**
- "Leños y Carbón for Colombian food or La Barra specifically for fried chicken. Both in elephant and castle."
- "Leños y Carbon is great but the service can be painfully slow. Lovely servers, tho."
- "Unfortunately I agree. You don't go to Colombian restaurants for the service hahah"

## La Barra
**Location:** Elephant and Castle

**Comments:**
- Specifically recommended for fried chicken
- Same location as Leños y Carbón

## Santa Fe
**Location:** Brixton Market

**Comments:**
- "Not Colombian, but I really like Santa Fe in Brixton Market."

## Paulas
**Location:** Brixton

**Comments:**
- "Paulas is great in Brixton for empanadas and pasteles"
- Also has a location in Elephant and Castle

## El Rancho de Lalo
**Location:** Near Acre Lane (moved from Brixton Market)

**Comments:**
- "El Rancho de Lalo is also decent near Acre lane"
- "They moved from the market to off acre lane, way bigger premises now."
- "Very fond memories of Rancho it was the first Colombian place I went to in london after moving here"

## Donde Carlos
**Location:** Shepherds Bush

**Comments:**
- Mentioned as a place to compare with other Colombian restaurants

## La Placita
**Location:** New Cross

**Comments:**
- "La Placita is small covered courtyard surrounded by lots of different food stalls. It's pretty busy Sunday afternoons with families and groups. Prices are good."
- "Not the greatest… my partner is colombian and we live 10 minutes from the Placita and we rarely go.. my daughter loves pandebonos and everytime we got some there were not fresh."

## Distriandina
**Location:** Elephant and Castle

**Comments:**
- "There is a lovely Colombian panaderia in Pechkam (Mil Delicias). Another nice place is Distriandina in Elephant, there is a shop and at the back you can have hot food (love menus there - we were there saturday and for £15 you get soup - sancocho - and a main + drink). Is in the same road as leno y carbon"

## Mil Delicias
**Location:** Peckham

**Comments:**
- Colombian panaderia (bakery)
- "There is a lovely Colombian panaderia in Pechkam (Mil Delicias)"

## Balboa
**Location:** Deptford

**Comments:**
- "Balboa down in Deptford for good Colombian Pacific cuisine."
